Quick decision guide

Foam core — waterlogged / heavy / cracked Check date first. Within 1 year + manufacturing defect = approve. Past 1 year OR outside force present = deny.
Vapor barrier — brittle / disintegrating Chemical / heat / off-gassing = deny. Early failure under 1 year = investigate further.
Vinyl tears / seam wear Cover lifter friction or strap misuse = deny. Clear stitching/manufacturing defect = approve.
Discoloration / yellowing underside Chemical, heat, off-gassing = deny. Not a manufacturing defect.
Wind / animal / snow / water pooling Always deny. Act of God / outside force — voids warranty.
Photos missing or incomplete Do not process. Send "Need more information" template and wait.
Clear manufacturing defect, within warranty period Approve. Document cause clearly before submitting replacement order.

Warranty policy reminder

"Foam core insert or foam core rib breakage or tearing of the outer skin material due to the cover being walked on, stood/sat or leaned upon or any excessive weight caused by rain, water, snow, animals, or any other acts of God will void the warranty."

Foam cores carry a 1-year limited warranty covering defects in materials or workmanship only.
